When deployed on Netlify, quiz submissions are automatically collected and can be viewed in your Netlify admin panel. Here's how to access them:

Step 1: Deploy your site to Netlify and ensure forms are working
Step 2: Log into your Netlify Dashboard
Step 3: Select your quiz site from the site list
Step 4: Navigate to Forms in the top navigation
Step 5: Click on quiz-submissions to view all form submissions
